About

When kicking off a new project, I start by collecting all the features and functionalities we need to implement. Then we design a prototype with Figma, track the project on GitHub, and deploy the app for production on Railway or Heroku. Mobile apps can be made available for download on the Play Store.

When I'm not developing, I teach at Le Wagon. This includes giving lectures on programming languages and frameworks, guiding students through daily coding challenges, and helping build apps and deploy them to production, as well as conducting online tutorials and workshops.

I'm always looking for opportunities to take on new challenges, so if you have an idea you want to see come to fruition, feel free to contact me. Look forward to collaborating with you!
