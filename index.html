Less is More

Less is More

A climate footprint application designed for mobile devices.

After taking a quick survey, see your CO2 impact compared to others in your country, then share ideas with users around the world on how to reduce your carbon footprint going forward.

FEATURES:

  • Built with Ruby on Rails (HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL)
  • Uses Devise for authentication/authorization
  • JavaScript for Mapbox globe functionality
  • Hammer.js for Tinder-like swiping cards
  • Stimulus to post messages
  • Deployed to Heroku

Computer Swap

Computer Swap

A marketplace Airbnb-style application for renting laptops.

Search for computers available in your area based on your requirements and book them for as long as you need.

FEATURES:

  • Built with Ruby on Rails (HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL)
  • Uses Devise for authentication/authorization
  • Searchbar functionality for choosing laptops
  • Mapbox to display the laptop pickup location
  • Bootstrap carousel-style images
  • Deployed to Heroku
