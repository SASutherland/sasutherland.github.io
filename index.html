Less is More

A climate footprint application designed for mobile devices.

After taking a quick survey, see your CO2 impact compared to others in your country, then share ideas with users around the world on how to reduce your carbon footprint going forward.

BUILT WITH:

  • Ruby on Rails (HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL)
  • Devise for authentication/authorization
  • JavaScript for Mapbox globe functionality
  • Hammer.js for Tinder-like swiping cards
  • Stimulus to post text messages
  • Deployed to Heroku
